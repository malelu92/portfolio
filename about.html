I'm a UX Designer with a background in Human-Computer Interaction and Computer Science. I work in collaboration with project managers, designers and engineers to create valuable and meaningful user experiences always having the user in the center of the process.

My path to UX Design wasn't always clear to me. When I started my undergrad all I knew was that I wanted to work on a field that mixed design and logic, but I was unaware of the existence of HCI and UX. After graduating in Computer Science and still looking for what I wanted to to, I took a mandatory Masters class that focused on UX and at that moment I knew I had found what I wanted to do. I'm passionate about emphatizing with the user and designing the best experience possible for people, always based on research data and usability tests.

Outside of work, I love traveling, learning languages, staying outdoors and playing sports. I've lived in 4 different countries and speak 4 languages.
